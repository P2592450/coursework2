Jumanji: The Next Level

Jumanji:The Next Level is a 2019 American fantasy adventure comedy directed by Jake Kasdan. It is a sequel to 2017's Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle, the second follow up to 1995's Jumanji, and is the fourth installment to the Jumanji Franchise. It stars Dwayne Johnson , Kevin Hart and Nick Jonas.

The film's plot takes place two years aftwe Welcome to the Jungle, in which the same group of teenagers, along with an old friend and two unwitting additions, become trapped in Jumanji. There, they all find themselves facing new problems and challenges with both old and new avatars while having to save the land from a new villiam in order to escape.
